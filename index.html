BLUE

Ah, blue. The color of boys, according to people who put lables on colors. (Come on, every color is for EVERYone). But back to blue.

In sports, I wore navy blue during my 3rd and 4th seasons of youth soccer. Naturally, it replaced regular blue on my list of "favorite colors" at the time. "I like green, orange and navy blue."
